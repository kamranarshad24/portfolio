I'm Kamran — a paid ads and tracking specialist based in Islamabad, Pakistan. I started by building my own e-commerce brand, AlphaMart, from zero — handling every part of the business myself, from product research and Shopify setup to running Snapchat ad campaigns that delivered a 5.69x ROAS and 1,034 purchases from just $1,452 in spend.

That hands-on experience taught me what most agencies miss: tracking has to be right before ads can scale. I've since worked with dental clinics, fashion brands, and a UK carpet cleaning business — building full tracking stacks (GTM, GA4, Facebook CAPI, server-side containers) and running Meta, Google, and Snapchat campaigns for each.

My strongest skill is finding what's quietly draining your ad budget — broken pixels, misconfigured events, split-attributed conversions — and fixing it before more money goes out the door. I work with clients across Pakistan and internationally, with dual PKR and USD pricing.
